Application

Who are you?
Enlistment denied.
Task Force Omega does not accept recruits under 16. Come back when you're old enough to follow orders.
Availability

We run operations twice a week — Thursday and Sunday evenings.
We don't expect you at every single op, but we do expect you to show up when you're available.

Mods

We run several modlists averaging 20 to 30 mods each.
You will need to install and keep them updated, all are provided.

Chain of command

TFO is a community first — everyone is a friend. That said, following orders during missions is non-negotiable.
It keeps operations running smoothly and keeps everyone alive.

Experience

Everyone goes through Basic Combat Training regardless of background.
We want all recruits starting from the same baseline — no exceptions.

How or where did you hear about us?
Trial op — optional

Want to jump in before committing? You're welcome to sit in on a live op as a guest.
