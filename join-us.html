Join Our Ranks

Follow these steps to become a full member of Task Force Omega

Recruitment

Step by step

  1. Join Discord & Apply

    Join our Discord server and apply to the @Recruits role on #recruitment-info

    (This will grant you access to the recruitment channel)

  2. Fill Out the Form

    Complete our recruitment form so we can get to know you better

    → Access the form to signup

  3. Schedule Your BCT

    Contact our @Drill Sergeants in #recruitment-center and schedule your Basic Combat Training

  4. Download the Modpack

    Get the required mods for your BCT

    → Download the BCT modlist

  5. Read Our Rules

    Familiarize yourself with our community guidelines and standards

    → Read the rules

  6. Complete Your BCT

    Successfully complete the Basic Combat Training with your assigned instructor

After Your BCT

  • Sign up for the Arma unit: units.arma3.com/unit/tfomega
  • Make sure your username and rank in Discord match in Arma (ex. PVT. Name)
  • Request access to the spreadsheet: docs.taskforceomega.eu
  • Sign up for missions and enjoy!

Modlist Setup

Installation guide

Installation Steps

  1. Open the Arma 3 Launcher
  2. Go to the Mods section
  3. Click on Preset, then click on Import
  4. Select the modlist file (.html) you downloaded
  5. Steam will download and enable all required mods automatically
